Top 3 Norwegian Cheeses

1. Kraftkar

Produced in: TORJULVÅGEN, NORWAY

Description: Produced from cow's milk from the family farm located in the village of Torjulvågen. Won the 2016 World Cheese Awards in San Sebastian, Spain.

2. Jarlsberg

Produced in: VESTFOLD COUNTY, NORWAY

Description: Has a semi-soft texture, while the flavors are mild, buttery, and slightly nutty. The cheese was invented by Anders Larsen Bakke in the village of Vale, not far away from Oslo.

3. Norzola

Produced in: OSLO, NORWAY

Description: This Norwegian blue cheese is made from pasteurized cow's milk, cream, salt, and rennet. Underneath its natural rind, the texture is buttery, smooth, and supple.
